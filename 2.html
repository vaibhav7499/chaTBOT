Examples of Urgent Complaints

Water Leak

There's a major water leak in the hostel bathroom on the second floor. Water is spreading to the hallway and electrical outlets.

Electrical Hazard

There's an electrical panel in the science building that's sparking and smoking. This seems dangerous and could start a fire.

Safety Incident

A student just had an accident in the chemistry lab and appears to be injured. This is an emergency situation requiring immediate attention.

Structural Issue

A large section of the ceiling in the library has collapsed. The situation is critical and unsafe for students studying nearby.

How the System Works

The Zeal College chatbot uses AI to scan your complaint description for keywords. If any urgent keywords are detected, your complaint is automatically flagged as high priority and sent to the facilities team for immediate action.

For the fastest response, include specific details about the nature and location of the problem in your complaint.
